# Contributor License Agreement process

Logit Loom accepts ordinary contributions under the inbound terms in
[`LICENSING.md`](LICENSING.md). Blackcat Informatics Inc. may require a
Contributor License Agreement before merging a substantial contribution.

The CLA permits the project to distribute contributed material under
`MIT OR Apache-2.0` and under separate proprietary terms. It does not transfer
ownership of the contributor's work unless the agreement explicitly says so.

When a CLA is required, a maintainer will provide the actual agreement and
signature process during review. This page describes the process; it is not
itself the agreement. Contributors should not include additional license terms
in a pull request unless they have first been accepted by the maintainers.
