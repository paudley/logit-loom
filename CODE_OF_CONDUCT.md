# Code of conduct

Logit Loom welcomes technically rigorous disagreement and expects participants
to treat one another with respect.

## Expected behavior

- Be clear, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas, code, evidence, and interfaces rather than people.
- Respect different backgrounds, communication styles, and levels of context.
- Accept correction, disclose uncertainty, and repair mistakes.
- Keep private information and security reports private.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory abuse, deliberate disruption, and unwanted
publication of private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, limit participation, or ban an
account when necessary to protect the project and its participants. Report a
conduct issue privately to a repository maintainer through GitHub. Reports will
be handled as confidentially as practical, with conflicts of interest routed to
another maintainer where possible.
